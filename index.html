How to Play

The Story

You are the keeper of an ancient telescope, lost in the void. Your mission is to rebuild its lenses and reveal the hidden constellations of the cosmos. But the stars are out of alignment.

The Rules

  • 1 The game is played on a 5x5 grid of lights.
  • 2 Tapping a light toggles it and its neighbors (creating a cross shape).
  • 3 Turn off all the lights to clear the board and collect Light Shards.
